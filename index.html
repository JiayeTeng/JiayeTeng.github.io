About Me

I am now a Ph.D student at Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University. I am very fortunate to have Professor Yang Yuan as my advisor. Previously, I did my undergraduate at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics, advised by Professor Yang Bai.

Research Interests

My interests involve exploring the statistical properties of mathematical models and how they can be used to explain real-world phenomena, particularly in the context of optimization theory and generalization theory in model training. Ultimately, my goal is to construct a general and fundamental mathematical framework that can help people better understand mathematical models, with the potential to make a significant impact in fields such as economics, finance and healthcare.
If you want to have a chat, always feel free to contact me through Email!
