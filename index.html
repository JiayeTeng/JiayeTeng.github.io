Selected Publications

Hyperparameter Optimization: A Spectral Approach,
        Elad Hazan, Adam Klivans, Yang Yuan, [α-β ordering]
        ICLR 2018. Previously appeared in NIPS DLTP Workshop 2017 (oral). [arxiv] [github]
        Details: we proposed a hyperparameter tuning algorithm using discrete Fourier analysis and compressed sensing. It tunes 39 hyperparameters for con-volutional neural networks in 6 days, and gets slightly better results than hand tuning. It is also the first algorithm for learning noisy decision tree with polynomial sample complexity. 

Convergence Analysis of Two-layer Neural Networks with ReLU Activation,
        Yuanzhi Li, Yang Yuan, [α-β ordering]
        NIPS 2017. [arxiv]
        Details: we provided a convergence analysis for SGD on a two layer feedforward network with ReLU activations and identity mappings. Under Gaussian input and initialization assumptions, we proved that SGD converges in two phases. 

Escaping From Saddle Points – Online Stochastic Gradient for Tensor Decomposition,
        Rong Ge, Furong Huang, Chi Jin, Yang Yuan, [α-β ordering]
        COLT 2015. [arxiv]
     Details: we identify the strict saddle property for non-convex problems, which guarantees that stochastic gradient descent will converge to a local minimum in polynomial time.
